Our Path So Far To PERFECTION

2011

Bringing A Vision Together

The company was started with one individual with a simple vision of providing clients and candidates with a better level of service in legal recruitment.

2012

Our 2nd expansion - Private Practice

We made our first step to growth by hiring additional members and by starting up our law firm recruitment function.

2013

Our 3rd expansion - Financial Services

We saw additional growth and expanded to handle legal & compliance within the financial services sector.

2014

Further growth with Accounting Recruitment Team

As part of our growth plans, we launched our accounting recruitment team. This came out of hard work, great passion for what we do and a vision to provide the same level of service to a greater number of clients and candidates.

2018

Further growth with Administative Recruitment

We grew our operations in 2018 to handle Admin & Support function positions to further service our clients.

2021

10th Year Anniversary

To mark our 10th year anniversary, we changed our name from Legal Intel to Alberto Recruitment. This is part of our plans to further expand our coverage area to better support clients in different sectors.

Our Recruitment Methodology

Our Unique recruitment methodology ensures clients receive the fastest and most efficient search possible. Rather than trying to fill every open position available in the market, we pride ourselves on working on a small number of job assignments at any given time to ensure our clients receive dedicated resources for their searches and the highest quality of work.
